Our Philosophy

Every piece begins as a conversation.
What matters to you becomes what we build.

The Craft

Built at the bench,
not on a screen

Every ring, pendant, and bracelet starts as raw metal and stone. We pour, forge, file, set, and polish by hand in our Austin studio. No 3D printing. No mass casting. The irregularities aren't flaws — they're proof a human made this.

How It Works

From Idea to Heirloom

01

Conversation

We sit down — in person or over a call — and talk about what you're envisioning. The metal, the stones, the occasion, the person who'll wear it. No commitment, no pressure.

02

Design & Forge

Sketches and material selection come first. Then the metalwork begins — casting, forging, shaping at the bench. You'll see progress photos along the way.

03

Delivery

Hand-finished, inspected, and delivered to you. Every piece comes with a certificate of authenticity and a lifetime of care from the person who made it.
